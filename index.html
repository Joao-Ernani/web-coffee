Our History

There wouldn't be so much coffee history if there weren't coffee shops. Thus, Turkey marks great importance in this trajectory. It can be said that the country was responsible for spreading the drink around the world, since it created the first coffee shop in 1475: the Kiva Han.

We are a coffee shop founded in the city of Montreal in 1945, known for its roasted coffee, which won the "best coffee in the world" award. And we are always innovating for your day to be better.
